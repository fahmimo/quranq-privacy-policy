Privacy Policy for QuranQ

Last updated:

At QuranQ, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains what information is handled by the app and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information
QuranQ does not require users to create an account. We do not collect personal identification information such as name or email address directly.

Usage Data
The app may store limited data locally on your device, such as quiz progress and prayer history, to support app functionality. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

Location Data
The app may request access to your location only to provide accurate prayer times. Location data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or shared with external servers.

2. Third-Party Services

QuranQ may include features that use third-party services that operate independently and have their own privacy policies:

  • WhatsApp — The “Contact Us” feature redirects you to WhatsApp. Any data shared during the chat is subject to WhatsApp’s privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — Typography may use Google Fonts, loaded according to Google’s standard practices.

3. Children’s Information

QuranQ does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child provided personal information within our app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

By using QuranQ, you hereby consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
